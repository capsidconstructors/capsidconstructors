Our Sponsors

We are pleased to acknowledge those who have supported us financially throughout this project, and hope that they will continue doing so with future BIOMOD teams.

We have received funding from the NSW Department of Trade and Investment through their Science and Engineering Student Competition Sponsorship Program.

In addition, we have been offered funding by various organisations within UNSW Sydney, including the Michael Crouch Innovation Centre, the UNSW Faculties of Engineering, Medicine and Science, the UNSW Schools of Biotechnology & Biomolecular Sciences and Physics, UNSW International and the student led group Arc@UNSW.

The European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL) Australia Node for Single Molecule Science is also one of our proud supporters. We are also generously provided with complimentary access to molecular biology software through our partnership with SnapGene.
